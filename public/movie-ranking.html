The Best Movies According to Angela

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

"The Shawshank Redemption" is a powerful drama that follows the story of Andy Dufresne, a wrongly convicted banker who finds hope and friendship while serving a life sentence in Shawshank State Penitentiary.

Inception

Directed by Christopher Nolan, this science fiction heist thriller stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals corporate secrets by entering people's dreams. Cobb is given a chance to regain his old life by planting an idea in someone's mind, but he must navigate a complex and dangerous dream world.

Pulp Fiction

Directed by Quentin Tarantino, this crime film weaves together multiple interconnected stories involving mobsters, hitmen, boxers, and other colorful characters in Los Angeles.
